ownership.

Offline Key Storage

Private keys are generated and stored exclusively on the Secure Element chip. They never connect to any network, are never transmitted, and never leave the device.

How It Works

Three steps to
full ownership.

From unboxing to fully secured crypto in under 10 minutes. No prior experience required.

I

Generate Your Keys

Your device arrives completely blank. During first setup, your 24-word seed phrase is generated directly on the Secure Element chip. It never leaves the device, never appears online, and we never see it.

II

Connect & Manage

Install the Lexchain companion app on your phone or desktop. Connect via Bluetooth or USB-C. View your portfolio, manage assets, and initiate transactions across all supported networks.

III

Confirm Every Transfer

When you send crypto, the transaction details appear on-screen. You must physically press the confirm button on the device. No remote action, no malware, no one else can approve a transfer on your behalf.
