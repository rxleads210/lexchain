How It Works

Three steps to
total security.

No setup complexity, no software installation, no dependency on cloud services.

I

Register

Plug in the key and click "Add security key" in your account's security settings. Your browser and the key generate a unique cryptographic key pair in seconds. The private key never leaves the device.

II

Authenticate

At your next login, enter your username. When prompted, touch the golden button on your Lexchain key. The device signs the challenge and returns it — phishing-resistant by design.

III

Stay Secure

Lexchain needs no batteries, no wireless pairing, and no software. It simply works — across thousands of services, on every modern operating system, every time.
